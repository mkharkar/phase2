Navbar

The navbar component consists of a dark grey background with a logo and name aligned to the left side, and a "Download Resume" button positioned on the right side.

Accessibility

The navbar is designed with clear color contrast and easy-to-read text to ensure accessibility for all users, including those with visual impairments.

Details

The navbar serves as a navigation tool for users to access different sections of the website. The logo and name provide branding and identity, while the "Download Resume" button offers a convenient way for users to access the resume document.
