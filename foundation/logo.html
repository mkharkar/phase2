Logo

Black and White

A black and white logo is presented for versatility, print adaptability, visual simplicity, accessibility, and brand consistency. It ensures clarity in various contexts and enhances the logo's practicality.

Inverse Black and White

The inverse black and white logo is employed on dark backgrounds for optimal visibility. This ensures clarity, maintains brand consistency, and enhances the logo's impact in situations with darker color schemes.

Colour Variation 1

Using the logo on a deep blue background illustrates its versatility and readability on dark backgrounds. It emphasizes the color scheme's adaptability, ensuring effective visual communication in diverse scenarios.

Colour Variation 2

Displaying the logo on a warm beige background demonstrates its adaptability and readability across light settings

Colour Variation 3

Showcasing the logo on a vibrant red background highlights its adaptability to colorful and dynamic environments. This ensures impactful visual communication in lively and vibrant contexts.

Favicon

A favicon logo is used for brand recognition and website identity. It appears in the browser tab, bookmarks, and other UI elements, providing a visual cue for users and enhancing the overall branding and user experience.
